Why should I use proxies for YouTube automation or scraping?
Yes, proxies are perfect for managing multiple social media accounts. Using mobile or residential proxies helps create unique IPs for each account, reducing the risk of detection or bans from platforms.
How do rotating proxies work?
On each request, rotating proxy endpoint assigns you a new IP address from your proxy list
What's the difference between a static residential proxy and a rotating residential proxy?
A static residential proxy gives you a fixed IP address, ideal for maintaining a consistent online identity. A rotating residential proxy automatically cycles through multiple IP addresses, mimicking natural browsing and reducing the risk of detection during high-volume tasks.
